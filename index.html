Tucor Bookmark Tools:
Export Controller Data to Excel

Use these one-click tools to export NLP data to an Excel file. Please follow the instructions below.

Export Programs to Excel

Receive a list of the Target Types and Names inside of each Program Step, along with Irrigation Type, Amount, and Wait Completion status.

1
Make sure your browser’s Bookmarks Bar is visible.
2
Drag the green button above to your Bookmarks Bar.
3
In the NLP, go to Irrigation Controller > Programs and click the Pencil icon on your desired program.
4
In the Programs menu, click on the Steps tab.
5
Click the bookmark to download the Excel file.

Export Station List to Excel

Receive a list of Station IDs, Station Names, and their corresponding Decoder IOs (Addresses).

1
Make sure your browser’s Bookmarks Bar is visible.
2
Drag the green button above to your Bookmarks Bar.
3
In the NLP, go to Irrigation Controller > Stations.
4
At the bottom of the screen, change Rows per page to All.
5
Click the bookmark to download the Excel file.
Note: This tool runs only in your browser. No data is uploaded or shared. Once the bookmark is clicked, an Excel file will be generated in your browser's downloads.
